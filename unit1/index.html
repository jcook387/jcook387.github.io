Magda, Brazen Outlaw cEDH

Dwarven tribal and artifact synergy

Reasons to play Magda:

  • Combos are something you enjoy.
  • Having a tool-box commander able to answer any threats that enter the board.
  • Having the ability to adapt between a combo strategy to a controlling strategy.

The main combo line:

Prerequisites:

  • Artifact dwarf or artifact that changes your dwarves to become an artifact
  • Clock of Omens card on battlefield
  • Magda, herself

Combo:

  1. Activate Clock of Omens to tap itself and the artifact dwarf.
  2. The tapped dwarf triggers Magdas ability, which creates an artifact treasure token.
  3. Clock of omens ability resolves, untapping the artifact dwarf.
  4. Now, using the Clock of Omens ability again, tap the artifact dwarf you untapped and the treasure token you generated from Magdas ability to untap the artifact dwarf.

This results in infinite tapped treasures, which we can use to activate Magda to search our deck for any artifact or dragon card and immediately goes to the battlefield.
